Identity & Values

This blog post will showcase my identity and values

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

I once got into an altercation with a coworker who was being physically aggressive to me. I had the decision to fight back or try to de-escalate the situation. I chose not to fight back as I knew that would only escalate the situation further and leave myself in a worse position in future. Some might say that this would make you look weak but for me there is nothing to gain from a fight and I am happy I made the decision to stay calm and avoid retaliation because the coworker did eventually calm down and the altercation never became serious.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

The culture and people around me have influenced my values in a way that I follow most of the social norms, and don’t cause much trouble.

Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

A strength is the ability to work through defined technical work and problem solve in the process. This will support me during my learning journey because I will be able to perform the technical side of the work more easily and have confidence doing so.

Another strength I have is the ability to troubleshoot. This will support my journey because at many times I will face errors or problems that I dont understand why is happening and I know how to exhaust all the options possible to find a solution.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

A limitation of mine is when the work I have to do becomes too open ended, e.g designing, or coming up with ideas for content or style of a project. This could affect my learning journey because many of the tasks will involve designing or coming up with ideas.

Another limitation I have could be the ability to communicate my thought process and my understanding with others. I mostly work on things myself so I have limited experience communicating my knowledge with others. This could affect my learning journey because I might need to explain verbally the concepts that I have learnt and the knowledge I have regarding the course material.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

An example is when playing team sports and your teammates are criticizing your actions despite trying to do the best thing in the moment. I might try to just focus on my game and ignore it or talk to a teammate about it. This strategy isn’t always effective so I think the better strategy is to make sure your teammates understand how you play and stamp out misunderstandings before the game.
