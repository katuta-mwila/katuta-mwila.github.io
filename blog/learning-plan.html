What is your long term goal or career pathway?

A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning

My strengths are the ability to research and troubleshoot problems using methods such as searching the web, debugging, and running tests on my local system. I will attempt to gain broader knowledge of what I'm working with to further my understanding of the problem I am running into.

Another strength is I do lots of research such as looking through documentation and videos about topics I am trying to understand before I try to implement to material.

A weakness I have is that I can often get distracted as on a computer you are only one tab away from being distracted by a youtube video, music, or other social media.

What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

A skill I would like to develop is the ability to collaborate with others on technical projects.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include: An explanation of how you will manage yourself to work productively and safely with other learners, Facilitators and industry/community representatives What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you

I plan to manage my workload by setting aside a time period in the day where I must be focused on studying. Outside of this time period should be the opposite, I should not be studying unless it is absolutely necessary. I will work productively with other learners by planning what work each of us should be doing and communicating clearly what I am working on and what work I have completed. When I am unsure of something I will ask my pears or facilitators for help.

When things are building up and starting to get to me I will take a short break and review all the things I have to do and in what timeframe. I will then set aside extra time in my week to help me complete my work. If there does not appear to be time I will talk to my facilitators about what can be done or if I could have some more time to complete my work.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

I will try and solve problems myself within a set period. If the time exceeds that period I will then ask facilitators for help.
