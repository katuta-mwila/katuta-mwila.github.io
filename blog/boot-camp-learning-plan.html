Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to get a career in tech and I hope to use the skills gained the bootcamp to achieve this. I don't have a specific destination in mind but any work involving websites or even other types of software is ideal.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

My biggest strengths in bootcamp will be my ability to understand the technical work and problem solve/trouble shoot where necessary.

My biggest limitation will probably be my creativity when it comes to ideas for more open ended projects or design.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

My biggest non-technical challenge and bootcamp could be keeping on top the material and staying focused or learning how to work effectively in team.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would like to develop the ability to maintain focus even when things get boring or hard. I would also like to develop the ability to work on projects in a team instead of solo.

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I expect that the bootcamp team are clear on exactly what they want from us and the work we are required to do. I also expect that they are able to help when we are stuck.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

My expectations of myself are that I complete all my work on time and don't get distracted.
